无 (no)辣 (spicy)不 (not)欢 (happy)

  • 我是无辣不欢的人。I can't enjoy food without spice.
  • 四川人基本都无辣不欢。Sichuan people basically need spice in everything.
  • 她无辣不欢，顿顿都要辣椒。She needs spice every meal.

Fixed colloquial expression. Good for describing someone who loves spicy food.
