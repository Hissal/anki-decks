True love is as vast as the grassland
Layer upon layer of wind and rain cannot block it
There always comes a time when the clouds part and the sun rises
Ten-thousand-zhang sunlight shines on you and me
True love is like plum blossoms that once bloomed
The coldest ice and snow cannot bury it
On the very coldest branch it bursts into bloom
Seeing spring walking toward you and me
Snowflakes drift, the north wind howls
Heaven and earth — a single vast expanse
A single sprig of cold plum stands proud in the snow
Only for that beloved one does it spread its fragrance
I love what I love, with no complaint, no regret
This feeling will linger in the heart forever
